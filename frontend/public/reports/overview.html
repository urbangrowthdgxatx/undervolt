Project Overview

UNDERVOLT revolutionizes urban planning by processing 2.3 million Austin building permits in real-time. By leveraging RAPIDS cuDF and the power of NVIDIA DGX Spark, we've transformed stagnant municipal data into a dynamic, queryable infrastructure engine.

Traditional CPU-based urban analytics often suffer from bottlenecking when processing decades of historical records. UNDERVOLT uses GPU parallelism to reduce complex spatial queries from minutes to milliseconds, enabling planners to visualize development trends instantaneously.
